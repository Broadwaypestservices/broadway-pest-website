The DOH Reality

Unannounced.
Unforgiving. Every time.

A→C
One Sighting = Grade Drop
NYC DOH inspectors score violations on sight. A live rodent or cockroach observation is 28 points — instantly dropping a clean kitchen from A to C. There is no warning. There is no grace period. There is only the grade that gets posted in your window within 24 hours of inspection.
48h
The Re-Inspection Window
If you receive a B or C grade, you have 48 hours to request a re-inspection. If you pass the re-inspection, you can post the A grade. This is your most critical recovery window — and why Broadway Pest is on call for pre-re-inspection emergency treatment the moment you call.

Revenue Impact
Research on NYC restaurant grades shows that a B grade reduces weekly covers by 12–18% on average. A posted C grade — even temporarily — can permanently affect Yelp and Google ratings, delivery platform rankings, and customer trust. Prevention costs a fraction of one week of reduced revenue.

Our restaurant IPM programs are built around your DOH inspection calendar, your kitchen operating hours, and the specific pest threats your location faces. Every product we use is food-service approved and can be applied without closing your kitchen.
