Class B and C pest violations, Local Law 55 IPM requirements, annual bed bug reporting, and 7A receivership defense documentation. Our programs are built to satisfy OATH administrative law judges.

Restaurant and food service inspection programs with on-site documentation. Our service records are formatted for immediate inspector review — the same day a Grade Pending card goes up.
