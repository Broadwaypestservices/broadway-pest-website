Our Story in Upper Manhattan

Five decades of staying power in this neighborhood.

We didn't just set up a location in Harlem — we grew up in upper Manhattan. Broadway Pest has been serving the families and businesses of this community since the early 1970s, long before it was fashionable.

When the rent on Amsterdam Avenue got too steep, we didn't leave the neighborhood. We moved a few blocks to St. Nicholas, because our customers are here and we're not going anywhere.
