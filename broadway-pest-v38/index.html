Our Doctrine

Pest control
is an ops
problem.

The NYC businesses that end up with DOH violations, HPD pest citations, and bad reviews aren't dealing with unusual pests — they're dealing with reactive extermination. German cockroaches, Norway rats, mice, bed bugs, and stored-product moths are manageable. Reactive, undocumented pest control in New York City is not. We operate differently: structured programs, digital logs, and same-day NYC exterminator response.

  • 01
    Assess before treating
    Every engagement starts with a thorough property assessment. We identify your specific vectors, vulnerabilities, and regulatory exposure before writing a single service order.
  • 02
    Document everything
    Every service generates a timestamped digital log. When the DOH inspector or HPD housing court asks for records, you have them — organized, complete, defensible.
  • 03
    Stay ahead of the threat
    Our IPM programs monitor, detect, and eliminate conditions before infestations establish — not after they're visible to tenants or inspectors.
  • 04
    Respond decisively
    Same-day dispatch for active infestations. Our Harlem and Midtown locations cover all five boroughs with no dead zones.
